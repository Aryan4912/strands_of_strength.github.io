## 💡 About the Project

Losing your hair to cancer treatment takes more than hair — it can take confidence, normalcy, and a sense of self. **Strands of Strength** exists to close that gap.

We connect three groups who don't usually meet:

- **🙋 Donors** — everyday people (and temples, a major source of hair donation in India) willing to donate their natural hair
- **🧵 Artisans & Manufacturers** — skilled wig makers who transform donated hair into natural, wearable wigs
- **💛 Patients** — cancer patients who receive an affordable, high-quality, natural wig instead of an expensive or synthetic alternative

Our mission is to make that entire chain — donation, craftsmanship, and delivery — **sustainable, ethical, and easy to be part of**, while raising awareness around hair donation as a simple, powerful act of generosity.

## 🚀 Running Locally

No build tools required — it's plain HTML/CSS/JS.

```bash
git clone https://github.com/Aryan4912/strands_of_strength.github.io.git
cd strands_of_strength.github.io
```

Then simply open `index.html` in your browser, or serve it locally:

```bash
python -m http.server 8000
```

Visit `http://localhost:8000` to view the site.
